GRANDMA'S BIRTHDAY

We also celebrated my grandma's birthday, making it a month full of good vibes and family time.

ANGARA CUP

April

I joined an introduction performance with my classmates for the Angara Cup kickoff at the CTU oval in Carmen, Cebu. It was a fun way to start the event, and performing together made it even more memorable.

COUSINS GET-TOGETHER

October

We had a fun cousin get-together at Apollo in Liloan, Cebu. It was a day filled with good food, non-stop laughs, and catching up on each other’s lives—it’s always a good time when we're all together

ALL SOULS' DAY

November

On All Souls' Day, I visited my departed loved ones at the cemetery. I spent some quiet time reflecting, lighting candles, and saying prayers—it was a simple but meaningful way to honor and remember them.

MANGROVE PLANTING

December

I took part in a mangrove planting activity in Tawsan, organized in collaboration with the ICPeP and IIEE organizations. It was a rewarding experience, helping the environment while working alongside friends and volunteers.
